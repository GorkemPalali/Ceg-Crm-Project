# NovaMart Customer Support FAQs

## Order Issues

**Q: I didn‚Äôt receive my order yet. What should I do?**

**A:** If your order hasn‚Äôt arrived by the expected delivery date (or says *delivered* but you can‚Äôt find it), here are steps to take:

1. **Verify your shipping details:** Double-check that your shipping address on the order is correct. Sometimes a typo or old address can cause misdelivery.
2. **Check for delivery attempts or notifications:** Look for any notice of attempted delivery (a slip on your door or an email). Also review the order status in your account for any updated delivery information.
3. **Inspect the delivery area:** Look around the usual drop-off locations at your home. Couriers may leave packages in a safe spot (porch, garage, back door) or with building management. Also check your mailbox (for smaller items).
4. **Ask neighbors or household members:** See if someone else accepted the package on your behalf. It‚Äôs common for neighbors or front-desk receptionists to hold onto a package for you.
5. **Wait 48 hours:** In some cases, packages might be marked as delivered *up to 48 hours* before they actually arrive. Carriers sometimes pre-scan items as delivered. Give it an extra day or two.
6. **Contact the carrier:** If 48 hours have passed, reach out to the delivery company (postal service or courier) with your tracking number to inquire about the package‚Äôs whereabouts. They might be able to provide details or coordinate a redelivery.
7. **Still no luck? Reach out for help:** If you still cannot locate the package after doing all the above, and especially if the order status shows delivered or long overdue, contact NovaMart Customer Service. We‚Äôll investigate the issue. For orders sold by third-party sellers on NovaMart‚Äôs marketplace, you can also contact the seller directly for assistance. In many cases, if a package is confirmed lost, we will arrange a replacement or refund for you.

**Q: How can I track my order status?**

**A:** Tracking your NovaMart order is easy:

- **Via the Website:** Log in to your account and go to **Your Orders**. Find the order you want to track and click **‚ÄúTrack Package‚Äù** (or **‚ÄúTrack Order‚Äù**) next to it. This will display the latest tracking updates, including the current location of the package and the estimated delivery date.
- **Via the Mobile App:** Go to the Orders section in the NovaMart app, select the order, and tap on the tracking option.
- **Tracking Details:** Once you open the tracking page, you‚Äôll see a history of scans (e.g., ‚ÄúShipped ‚Äì In Transit ‚Äì Out for Delivery ‚Äì Delivered‚Äù). Updates may include the carrier name and a tracking number. If your order has multiple items or packages, each may have its own tracking information and delivery date.
- **No Tracking Available?:** If you don‚Äôt see a tracking number, it could be an order from a third-party seller or a digital item. For marketplace orders, the seller might not have provided tracking immediately ‚Äì give it a bit of time or contact the seller if needed. For any delivery without tracking, you‚Äôll at least see an estimated delivery window in Your Orders.
- **Notifications:** NovaMart will also send shipment confirmation emails with tracking links. You can opt into push notifications in the app to get real-time updates on your delivery.

**Q: Can I cancel or change my order after it‚Äôs been placed?**

**A:** It depends on the order status, but we‚Äôll guide you through the options:

- **Order Cancellation (before shipment):** If your order hasn‚Äôt shipped yet, you can cancel it online. Go to **Your Orders**, find the order, and select the option to **Cancel Items**. Choose the items you want to cancel (or the whole order) and confirm. You‚Äôll receive a confirmation email if cancellation is successful. (If you don‚Äôt see a cancel option, the order may have already processed for shipment.)
- **Changing Address or Details (before shipment):** Unfortunately, you *cannot edit* the shipping address or payment method on an order once it‚Äôs placed. The only workaround is to cancel the order (if possible) and place a new one with the correct details. This is especially true for marketplace orders from third-party sellers ‚Äì NovaMart doesn‚Äôt support modifying the address after purchase; you would need to request a cancellation and reorder with the correct address.
- **Too Late to Cancel (already shipped):** If your order is in the shipping process or already shipped out, we cannot cancel or alter it. In this case, you have a couple of options:
    - Wait for the package to arrive, then **initiate a return** if you don‚Äôt want the item. NovaMart‚Äôs return policy will allow you to send it back for a refund in most cases.
    - If it was a marketplace item, you can also message the seller to let them know you‚Äôd like to return it once received.
- **Partial Cancellation:** If you ordered multiple items and only want to cancel one of them, our system allows that as long as that item isn‚Äôt shipped yet. Go to Cancel Items and check off just the items you wish to cancel.
- **Changing an Order (after receipt):** If you want a different size or color, or you missed adding something, you can‚Äôt change an existing order that‚Äôs already processing. Instead, you can place a new order for the new item and, if needed, return the original item.
- **Contact Support if Needed:** If you‚Äôre unable to cancel online (for example, the item is in a stage where online cancellation is closed), you can reach out to NovaMart support for assistance. We can sometimes help prevent a shipment if it‚Äôs extremely soon after ordering, but once it‚Äôs in the hands of the carrier, we can‚Äôt stop it. So act quickly via the website for the best chance.

## Payment Problems

**Q: I was charged twice for my order. What should I do?**

**A:** Seeing two charges can be concerning, but in most cases there‚Äôs an explanation:

- **Split Shipments:** First, check if your order was split into multiple shipments. NovaMart (and our sellers) charge when each item ships. So if one order ships in two parts, you‚Äôll see two separate charges on your card, but the **total** should equal the correct amount for your order. This is normal and not an extra charge. You can verify by matching the amounts to items shipped.
- **Authorization Hold:** If the duplicate charge is for the exact same amount and on the same day, one of them might be a pending *authorization*. When you place an order, NovaMart requests payment authorization from your bank. If an order is edited or the first attempt had an issue, multiple authorizations can appear. These pending holds **are not final charges** and should disappear from your statement in a few days.
- **Double Charge Error:** If you‚Äôve confirmed that you have truly been charged twice for the *same* order (and not due to the above scenarios), here‚Äôs what to do:
    - Contact your bank or credit card to ensure one charge isn‚Äôt still pending. Sometimes banks can explain if one is just an authorization.
    - Reach out to NovaMart Customer Service with your order number and proof of the double charge. We will investigate and **refund any duplicate charge** promptly.
- **Check Order History:** It‚Äôs also helpful to log in to NovaMart and view your Transactions or Order History. Make sure there wasn‚Äôt a duplicate order placed by mistake. If two identical orders were made, you‚Äôd need to cancel one or return it.
- **Time Frame:** If a duplicate charge (that shouldn‚Äôt be there) actually went through, NovaMart will work with you to fix it. Refunds for billing errors are typically processed quickly once confirmed. If you ever notice an unexpected charge, let us know. We want to ensure you only pay for what you purchased, nothing more.

- **Cancelled Order:** If you cancelled an order before it shipped, you‚Äôll automatically be refunded in full. **Q: My payment was declined. How can I complete my order?**

**A:** If your payment didn‚Äôt go through, don‚Äôt worry ‚Äî here‚Äôs what you can do:

- **Check the Basics:** First, double-check that you entered your card details correctly. An incorrect card number, expiration date, CVV code, or billing ZIP code can cause declines. Ensure the billing address you provided matches what your bank has on file for that card.
- **Sufficient Funds/Credit:** Make sure your account has enough funds or credit limit available for the purchase. Sometimes a card is declined if the purchase exceeds your available balance or credit limit.
- **Contact Your Bank (if needed):** Banks have security measures that might block a transaction if it looks unusual (e.g., a large amount or an online order from a new retailer). If your card details are correct but the payment is still declined, call the customer service number on the back of your card. Ask if there are any holds or blocks on your card. They can often tell you the reason (expired card, suspected fraud, etc.) and help clear the issue. After confirming with your bank, you can try the payment again.
- **Retry the Payment:** After addressing any potential bank issues, go back to NovaMart and **retry the payment**. If the order is still open in your account (with status ‚ÄúPayment declined‚Äù or ‚ÄúPayment to retry‚Äù), you should see an option to **update payment method** or **retry**. For example, in **Your Orders** there may be a **‚ÄúRetry Payment‚Äù** button for that order ‚Äì click it and either re-enter the same card or choose a different payment method.
- **Alternative Payment Method:** If the issue persists with one card, try using another card or payment method (if you have one available, such as PayPal, if NovaMart supports it, or a different credit/debit card). This can quickly determine if the problem is with the specific card.
- **During Checkout vs. After:** If the decline happened during checkout and you haven‚Äôt placed the order yet, simply start the checkout again and use the corrected or new payment method. If the order is in a pending state in your account awaiting payment, use the instructions above to update the payment.
- **Keep an Eye on Email:** NovaMart will usually send an email if a payment is declined, with instructions. We typically give a window (e.g. 24-48 hours) to fix the payment issue before the order is automatically cancelled. So don‚Äôt delay too long.
- **Still Having Trouble?** If you‚Äôve tried all the above and your payment still won‚Äôt go through, contact NovaMart support. We can help identify if there‚Äôs an account issue or a site issue. Occasionally, high security settings on a card require the bank to hear directly from you before approving (especially for international transactions). Once resolved, you can complete your purchase. We‚Äôre here to assist so you can successfully place your order.

## Account Management

**Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**

**A:** Don‚Äôt worry, it happens to all of us. Resetting your NovaMart password is straightforward:
